Livrari la Domiciliu would like to make sure you are fully aware of all your data protection rights. Every user is entiteled to the following:

  • Right to erasure - You have the right to request the erasure of your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • The right to restrict processing - You have the right to request the restriction of processing your persona data, under certain conditions
  • The right to object to processing - You have the right to object to the processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • The right to data protability - You have the right to request the transfer of collected data to another organization, or directly to you, under certain conditions.
